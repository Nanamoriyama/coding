JAPANSE LES IN AMS

Our lessons:

At my school, we embrace diversity and understand that every learner is unique. I teach students of all levels(N5 to N1), from beginners taking their first steps into the world of Japanese to advanced learners striving for fluency. One important thing to remember is that everyone’s learning speed is different.

Our Approach:

I believe in tailoring your Japanese learning experience to suit your individual pace and preferences. Together, we’ll set achievable goals, and I’ll be there every step of the way to guide you through your language adventure. Your comfort is key, and I’m here to ensure your learning experience is as enjoyable as it is effective. We’ll work at a pace that suits you best, making sure you feel confident and motivated throughout your language learning journey.

Nana

Konnichiwa! I’m Nana, and I am from Osaka, Japan. With my passion for Japanese, I’ve spent 7 years teaching language and culture in the Netherlands.
My journey as a Japanese language instructor began in the Netherlands, Over these 7 years, I’ve honed my teaching skills and developed a deep understanding of the needs of learners at all levels.

For me, teaching Japanese is not just a profession; it’s a way to foster connections, one student at a time. I believe in the power of personalized learning experiences, tailored to the unique needs and preferences of each student.

Your satisfaction with my lessons is important. I take great pride in creating a positive and encouraging learning environment, where you not only learn the language but also feel a strong sense of achievement and enjoyment throughout the process.
